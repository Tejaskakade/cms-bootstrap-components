Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the finest batsmen in cricket.

Mumbai Cha Raja

Rohit Sharma made his debut for the Indian cricket team in One Day Internationals (ODIs) against Ireland in 2007.

Hitman

Rohit Sharma holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs, with 264 runs against Sri Lanka in 2014.
